Only The King Can Do It Ministry

Evangelist Hawkins is anointed and called by Christ Jesus as a soul winner for the Kingdom of God. She has a passionate and sincere love for God and all of His people. Humbly, Evangelist Daffney is a powerful and prolific orator. Her charismatic messages are laced with love, truth and excitement, which penetrate within the hearts of men and women. In addition to her speaking engagements, she is a conference host and an outreach counselor.
Only the King Can Do It Ministry, Inc. (OKCDIM) was established in the summer of June 2008. The purpose of the ministry is to equip, encourage, inspire, mobilize and motivate individuals to reach their spiritual and natural accomplishments. Evangelist Hawkins motto is "Never Give Up"! The mission serves the Body of Christ and all others, with the authentic restoration power of God's love and His divine revelations.
Evangelist Daffney Hawkins is a woman on the move.
Currently, she is enrolled in community college seeking a degree in Psychology. She holds a Bachelor of Ministry Degree from the Freedom Seminary and Bible College and has received two honor awards: the Dean's list and Magna Cum Laude. Daffney has three sons Toney, Travis, Teron and one daughter-in-love Brenda.
